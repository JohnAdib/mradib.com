# Playbook: adding or editing an article

Articles prove John thinks at the industry level, not just executes.

## Two article types, different jobs

1. Authority pieces: opinionated takes on AI-first engineering, leadership,
   and the future of the craft. These build the leader frame. Prioritize
   them.
2. How-to guides: practical, generous, SEO strong (the resume guide is the
   model). These bring search traffic and prove depth.

## Framing rules

- Titles make a claim, not a description. A reader should sense a point of
  view before clicking.
- Open with the thesis in the first two sentences. No warm-up paragraphs.
- Connect to the talks: an article that extends a talk links to it, and the
  talk links back.
- An article earns a homepage mention only if it serves the primary
  audience's wow; otherwise it lives on /articles.

## SEO

- Target a phrase people actually search; put it in the title, the
  description, and the first heading.
- OG image required per playbooks/seo.md.
- Add the article to src/data/articles/articles-meta.ts; RSS, sitemap, and
  llms.txt derive from it.

## Checklist

- [ ] Title carries a point of view
- [ ] Thesis in the first two sentences
- [ ] Cross-links to a related talk or article
- [ ] Metadata, OG image, JSON-LD set
- [ ] Facts traceable, numbers verified against docs/
