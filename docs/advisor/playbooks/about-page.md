# Playbook: About page

The About page is the story. The homepage earns the click; About delivers
"what an amazing story".

## Structure rules

- Chapters, chronological, each ending stronger than it began. The current
  12-chapter life story is the right shape; protect it.
- Open with a hook, never with "John is an Engineering Manager". The first
  paragraph promises a story worth reading.
- Each chapter carries one wow beat where the facts allow: the teenage
  WorldSkills medalist, teaching 2,000+ students, the $1M raise and the
  million-user month, growing Jibres to 1,200+ businesses, the move to
  London, the government endorsement, the #1 mentor ranking, the stages.
- Close looking forward (AI-first, what comes next), never with a summary.

## Voice notes for this page

- First person or close third, warm but certain. This is the one page where
  the human being shows: family in prose, the why behind each move.
- Struggle is allowed and valuable. The arc means more when the reader sees
  what it cost. Setbacks are framed as setup for the next rise.

## Checklist before shipping a change

- [ ] Does the opening hook survive the 5-second test?
- [ ] Does every chapter end with rising momentum?
- [ ] Is every fact traceable to docs/profile or docs/speaking?
- [ ] Are images placed at the wow beats (see imagery.md)?
- [ ] Metadata and JSON-LD updated to match (see playbooks/seo.md)?
